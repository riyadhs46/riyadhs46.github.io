About Palma Sol

Est. 1998

Born from a love of paradise

Palma Sol was born in 1998 when founders Elena and Rafael Cruz discovered a hidden stretch of coastline and knew it had to be shared with the world — carefully, thoughtfully, and beautifully.

What began as 6 beachfront bungalows has grown into an award-winning resort spanning 12 lush acres, with 48 handcrafted villas and suites, three restaurants, a world-class spa, and a team of 120 passionate hosts.
